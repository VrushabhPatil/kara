About Us

Established in 2016, KARA International Logistics is a premier provider of comprehensive logistical solutions across India. We specialize in handling part load, full truckload (FTL), e-commerce, export, and import shipments. Our team of seasoned professionals boasts decades of experience in transportation and warehousing, ensuring that we deliver exceptional service to our valued customers.

Our Growth

Over the years, KARA has grown significantly in the domestic transportation sector. Our expertise allows us to meet all logistical requirements with proficiency and reliability, making us a trusted name in the industry.

Our Values

At KARA, we foster a culture of transparency and open communication, promoting a healthy employee-employer relationship. We believe in building strong trust relationships to create a highly integrated workplace with dedicated, customer-focused employees.

Leadership and Innovation

We believe in effective leadership with high accountability standards and actively support innovation. Our employees are committed to continuous learning and skill development. We strive to implement the latest performance-based compensation and reward programs to motivate our team.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced Team: Decades of experience in transportation and warehousing.

  • Comprehensive Solutions: Expertise in part load, FTL, e-commerce, export, and import shipments.

  • Customer Focused: Dedicated to serving our esteemed customers with transparency and reliability.

  • Innovation Driven: Committed to continuous learning and implementing the latest industry practices.
